Wed 07 August 2024

Let's Begin the Journey

I've been thinking for a few weeks about starting my own blog. It's not so much about entertaining people as it is about having someone keep an eye on what I do. In other words, I want to put some pressure on myself to finish my projects. I've had plenty of instances where I've had a big idea, done my research, and bought all the materials, only to end up with a box in my cold, dark cellar. So, to make this work better, I'm going to document my work on this blog.

Additionally, it will give myself some kind of documentation :P.
